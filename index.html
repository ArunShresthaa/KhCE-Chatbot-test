Welcome to Khwopa

KhCE is community-based engineering college, undertaken by Bhaktapur Municipality. It is centrally located at culturally rich city Bhaktapur. KhCE will, in every regard, be the right destination for those who aspire to become professional engineers at affordable fees.

Scholarships

#1 - Entrance Topper

10% of the total admitted students in each program will receive a scholarship based on their IOE entrance rank.

#2 - Semester Topper

Full tuition fee waiver for the semester topper based on IOE’s final exam result. If multiple toppers exist, the amount is divided.

#3 - Diligent & Needy

The college may provide scholarships to financially needy and hardworking students.
